About

50% of mental illness cases manifest in kids by the age of 14, according to the National Institute of Mental Illness.

However, many students' first exposure to mental health education is in high school.

Meet Bubble: An uncomplicated learning resource designed to introduce kids to mental health topics.

We want to know

What can we do to teach the importance of mental health in an engaging, informative way - all to elementary age students?

Our Mission:

We promote the normalization of mental health through inclusive, stigma-free education for all.
Through our unique methods, we encourage discussion, tolerance, and awareness for kids.
