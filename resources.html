Resources

Here we've compiled a list of the best resources for kids and parents alike to learn more about mental health.
About Mental Illness

Bring Change to Mind is a simple, informative site that provides information on every form of metal illness and gives pointers on how to handle it.



Children's Health Blog

Kingsfund is a website/organization especially directed in helping people under the age of 21.



No Stigma Allyship Program

No Stigma is an organization that provides an ally program, complete with training, discussion groups, and volunteer opportunities.



NIMH Resources for Children

This page on the National Institute of Mental Health webpage is directed towards parents, and helping them notice signs of mental distress in their kids.



Recognizing Mental Health Issues in Children

Mental Health America also assists parents identify warning signs for their children, and offers places to get help.
