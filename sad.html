You might feel sad after you lose something that's really important to you, or when you feel like you have lost something important.


When people are sad, they sometimes cry or distance themselves from others.


Sadness is not a bad feeling. It can help you process your emotions better and it gives you the space to continue or make a new path for yourself.


Sadness might also help you connect with others. If someone you love is feeling sad, you can be there to support them. This will make them feel better, and you will be a good friend to them.


It's important that you recognize your sadness instead of pushing it away, or trying to cover it up with another emotion, like anger.


Sometimes, sadness doesn't feel like sadness. You can feel really really angry or even nothing at all when you're sad.


Talking or spending time with the people you love can make you feel more positive when you're sad. Playing with your favorite games or eating your favorite food can also be a way to deal wiht the sadness.
